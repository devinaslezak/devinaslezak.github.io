Battery Thermal Monitoring System — Aztec Electric Racing

Role: Energy Storage Team Member

I supported the development of a temperature sensing solution for battery pack monitoring on the Aztec Electric Racing Formula SAE electric vehicle. The goal of the project was to improve thermal visibility inside the energy storage system and provide better data for safe and reliable vehicle operation.

My work focused on sensor placement, mechanical integration, and packaging near battery cells while maintaining compatibility with existing system constraints. This required attention to space limitations, serviceability, wiring paths, safety considerations, and data collection needs.

The system improved the team’s ability to monitor battery temperature behavior, supporting safer battery operation and better performance analysis during testing and vehicle development.

Tools & Skills: Battery systems, thermal monitoring, sensor integration, packaging, system constraints, data analysis, safety-focused design
